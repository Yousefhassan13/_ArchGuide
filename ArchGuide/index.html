About

Welcome to ArchGuide!

- We are a community of architecture students from different colleges and universities all over Egypt. We are passionate about architectural design, innovation and creativity.
- ArchGuide is your go-to hub where you can find everything you will need to enrich your knowledge-seeking mind with interesting architecture data.
- Our platform helps you learn more about the most important terms related to architecture and keep you updated about the most useful scientific reference books, online courses, workshops, events and student activities.
- Our Mission is creating a community for architecture students, recent graduates and all design enthusiasts where they can share everything related to architecture, exchange knowledge and information and connect together to explore new ingenious ideas.
- Our vision is to be the best and the first go-to platform from helping all students, architects and designers to enrich their knowledge, learn more and find any information they might look for about architecture here on our platform.
- Our team is always in your help to answer all your questions about the architecture field if you have any!
- Whether you’re a student, recent graduate, or an architect, follow us and connect with like-minded individuals that will help you learn, grow, and improve your architectural thinking! Join us and be part of our exciting journey!
